History & Purpose

The Association of International Students (AIS) was founded to unite international students at YunTech. Serving as the official cultural facilitator and event-organizing arm of the Office of International Affairs (OIA), we are deeply focused on cultural integration, student wellbeing, and fostering professional and network development.

Our Vision

To become YunTech's leading global facilitator organization — bridging cultural diversity, developing the professional capacity of international students, and supporting the internationalization goals of the Office of International Affairs through inclusive, agile, and financially sustainable event management.

Our Mission

To excel in field execution, optimizing budget use and reducing bureaucratic redundancy through:

  • Digital Expansion & Visibility: Transforming event publicity into measurable social media campaigns that strengthen YunTech's international profile and help attract prospective global students.
  • Inclusivity & Leadership Succession: Designing a volunteer framework that allows students from diverse countries and academic years to participate dynamically and grow into future leaders.

AIS proudly aligns with YunTech's official core values: Sincerity, Honor, Perseverance, and Originality, while actively supporting the UN Sustainable Development Goal 17 (Global Partnerships).
